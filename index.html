Welcome to Choice Cuts

The Choice Cuts: Cebu and the Pork Barrel is a project of the Sun.Star Cebu Central Newsroom and aims to track the Priority Development Assistance Fund (PDAF) of Cebu legislators using publicly-available data, newsroom archives and the help of contributors.

Authors and Contributors

The project is done by journalists of Sun.Star Cebu led by Business Editor Max Limpag (@maxlimpag) and editor-in-chief Isolde Amante (@isoldeamante).
