<h1 class="name">Benhur Salimbangon</h1>
<h3 class="district">Cebu Province, Fourth District</h3>
			
<p>This is supposed to be Rep. Benhur Salimbangon's third consecutive term as a member of the House of Representatives. The Supreme Court, however, ruled in early 2010 that Salimbangon had actually lost the May 2007 election to then Bogo City Mayor Celestino Martinez III. By then, Salimbangon had served nearly the entire 2007-2010 term. He again defeated Martinez in 2010 and 2013. Before joining Congress, Salimbangon served on the Cebu Provincial Board. </p>

<p><strong>Terms:</strong>: 2010-2013, 2013-2016</p>
<p><strong>Total PDAF:</strong> P196,500,000</p>
