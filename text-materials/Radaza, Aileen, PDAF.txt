AILEEN RADAZA
Lapu-Lapu City, Lone District

Aileen Radaza is a first-time public official who succeeded her father, Arturo Radaza, as representative of the lone district of Lapu-Lapu City. Her term started last June 30, 2013.
As of early October, the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) has not reported any release from Radaza's Priority Development Assistance Funds (PDAF). The Supreme Court restrained all PDAF releases in an order last September.
