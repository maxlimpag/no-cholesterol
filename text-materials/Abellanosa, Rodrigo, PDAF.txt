RODRIGO ABELLANOSA
Cebu City, South District

After three terms in the Cebu City Council, Rodrigo Abellanosa ran for and won a seat in the Lower House, representing the city's south district. His first term started last June 30, 2013.
The Department of Budget and Management (DBM) has yet to report any release from Abellanosa's Priority Development Assistance Funds (PDAF).
