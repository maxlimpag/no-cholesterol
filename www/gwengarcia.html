Gwendolyn F. Garcia

Cebu Province, Third District

Gwendolyn Garcia served three terms as Cebu governor (2004 to 2013), before winning her first term as congresswoman of Cebu Province's third district in the May 2013 elections.

The Supreme Court, in September 2013, restrained the use of all Priority Development Assistance Funds (PDAF) for the year. The Department of Budget and Management (DBM) has not reported any release from Garcia's PDAF yet.
